Rick Riordan

An author that plays with the gods

"It's not bad to be different. Sometimes, it's the mark of being very, very talented." - Rick Riordan

Facts and Trivia about Rick Riordan:

  • Riordan, born in 1964, is a graduate from University of Texas at Austin, with two majors for English and history.
  • Yet his early works, like the mystery series of Tres Navarre, were directed to adults, Riordan really made his name with "Percy Jackson and the Olympians" series.
  • Most of Riordan's series is based on a certain mythology. So far he worked with Greek, Roman, Egypt and Norse folklore, creating characters that actually interact with gods from the myths.
  • Nicknamed as Uncle Rick by his fans, Riordan created the first Percy Jackson book in order to encourage his son to read. The main character is actually based on Haley Riordan himself, having the same traits of ADHD and dyslexia.
